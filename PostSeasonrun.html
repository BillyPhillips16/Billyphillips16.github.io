The Post-season run

Index

The Post-season run for the Redsox was a breeze.

Their first opponent was the New York Yankees, who they went on to beat 3-1 in a best of 5 series.

Their second opponent, in the American League Championship series was the Houston Astros, who they went on to beat 4-1 in a best of 7 series.

Their final opponent, who they played in the World Series, was the Los Angeles Dodgers, they went on to win that series 4-1 in a best of 7 series.

The Redsox were able to put up a dominant showing without some of their key players, some of those players are:

  • Dustin Pedroia, who has missed considerable time with a bad knee.
  • David Wright, a knuckleballer who has had a lot of struggles with shoulder pain in the passed couple of years.
  • Tyler Thornburg, a hard throwing reliever who had been dealing with elbow pain throughout the year.

    • Despite the absence of those players, they still were able to be in complete control in each series they played in.
