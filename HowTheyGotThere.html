How they got to the post-season

Index

The Redsox came out hot to start the season, but the Yankees did as well.

Midway through the season, the Redsox found themselves in second place, trailing no other than the New York Yankees.

Then the Redsox got hot, and took over first place and never looked back the rest of the season.

It was a tough race to finish, as the Redsox play in the toughest division in baseball, the American League East.
